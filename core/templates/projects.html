IRRBB Tool

Interest Rate Risk in the Banking Book Calculation

An international project where I created a tool to calculate
interest rates depending on the different shock scenarios.

  • I used pandas in the backend to perform very complex calculations.
    The previous painful calculation process was simplified with minimal
    user inputs and a friendly UI.
  • Django is the preferred library for the backend because of its fast
    development pace and integration with data manipulation libraries.
