Toilet Reviews

Every toilet has a life experience within.

Method

I utilized the analytical hierarchy process (AHP) to compute the coefficients for each criterion. I grade the toilets on a scale of 1 to 10 for each criterion, and then I multiply the ratings by the coefficients. After adding all the products, I calculate the ultimate score for each toilet.
